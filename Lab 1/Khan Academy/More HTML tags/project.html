Recipe #1

"Greek salad"

Ingredients Quantity
Olive oil ¼ cup
Cucumber 1 piece
Green bell pepper 1 piece
Cherry tomatoes 2 cups
Feta cheese 5 ounces
Olives ⅓ cup
  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 3-4 people

Step 1: Arrange the cucumber, green pepper, cherry tomatoes, feta cheese, red onions, and olives.

Step 2: Drizzle with the dressing and very gently toss.

Step 3: Season to taste and serve.

Source: Greek salad

Recipe #2

"Caesar Salad"

Ingredients Quantity
Romaine lettuce 2 heads
Parmesan cheese 200g
Crisp croutons 200g
Cherry tomatoes 2 cups
  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 3-4 people

Step 1: Combine all of your ingredients and toss gently to coat the lettuce in caesar dressing

Source: Caesar salad
